Our Mission

Built for Southeast Texas. By Southeast Texas.

SETX Beacon exists to bring neighbors together and build meaningful connections across Southeast Texas through local events and shared service opportunities. We create welcoming, low-barrier spaces where people can gather, build relationships, and experience a true sense of belonging.

By strengthening community ties and supporting connection across diverse groups, we help foster a more engaged, resilient, and connected region.

Who We Are

Organization Overview

SETX Beacon is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization headquartered in Beaumont, Texas. We exist to bridge divides, foster meaningful dialogue, and build the connective tissue that makes a community whole.

Our work focuses on creating low-barrier, welcoming spaces — events, gatherings, and service opportunities — where people from all walks of life can meet, form relationships, and become part of something bigger than themselves.

Our Journey

History & Milestones

2024

The Idea Takes Root

Founders identified a gap in Southeast Texas: a lack of accessible, welcoming community infrastructure that could bridge diverse groups. SETX Beacon was conceived as a solution — a new light for a region that deserved better connection.

2025 — Early

Official Formation

SETX Beacon was formally incorporated. Leadership structure was put in place and the organization's core mission, values, and programs were defined. Operations were established at 1045 Church St, Beaumont, TX.

2025 — Mid

First Community Events

We hosted our inaugural community gatherings across Southeast Texas, reaching hundreds of residents and establishing our first local partnerships with community organizations.

2025 — Q4

Brand & Digital Launch

Developed our full brand identity and launched our digital presence. Expanded event programming and community outreach across the region.

December 24, 2025

IRS 501(c)(3) Approval

The Internal Revenue Service formally recognized SETX Beacon as a tax-exempt public charity under IRC Section 501(c)(3). Contributions to SETX Beacon became fully tax-deductible as of this date.

February 2026

Determination Letter Received

Official IRS Determination Letter (Letter 947) issued February 24, 2026. SETX Beacon is now fully recognized and operating as a federally tax-exempt public charity.

2026 — Now

Growing the Beacon

Continuing to expand partnerships, programs, and reach across Southeast Texas. Pursuing Google for Nonprofits and additional resources to scale community impact.

Budget Narrative

How We Will Use Donations

🎉

Events & Programming (60%)

The majority of all funds go directly to community events: venue coordination, event supplies, food and refreshments, and facilitator costs. This is the core of what we do.

📣

Outreach & Comms (20%)

Digital marketing, social media, print materials, and community outreach to ensure our programs reach the people who need them most across Southeast Texas.

⚙️

Operations & Admin (12%)

Basic organizational costs: legal filings, accounting, insurance, and minimal administrative overhead. We keep this intentionally low as a commitment to our community.

Our Commitment to Transparency

SETX Beacon is committed to operating with full financial transparency from day one. As our organization grows and receives its first donations, we will publish annual reports, make our Form 990 filings publicly available, and maintain open books for all stakeholders and community members.

Questions about our finances or operations? Email us at info@setxbeacon.org. We respond to all financial inquiries.
