Who We Are

A new kind of civic organization.

SETX Beacon is an IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit rooted in Southeast Texas. We are not a government agency, a political group, or a social services provider. We are a community organization focused entirely on bringing people together and making it easier for residents to show up, connect, and take part in the life of their region.

We believe a stronger Southeast Texas starts with neighbors who know each other. Everything we do is designed to make that happen.

About SETX Beacon

Who We Are

We close the gap between wanting to help and knowing how.

BEACON is a Southeast Texas nonprofit focused on civic participation and community connection. We started with a simple observation: people want to be involved, but many do not feel invited, informed, or connected enough to take that first step.

Our role is to close that gap. We create spaces where people can show up, learn, meet others, and start getting involved in ways that feel natural and accessible.

What Guides Us

Our Values

Welcome & Respect

Real Participation

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2025 / Early

Where This Started

This work started through local organizing where neighbors were helping neighbors understand voting, local issues, and how to get involved.

2025 / Mid

The Gap Became Clear

We saw the real barrier was not interest. It was connection. People wanted to show up but did not always know how or where.

2025 / Mid

BEACON Was Created

BEACON was created to focus on that gap: building community spaces and making civic participation more accessible.

2025 / Late

Formally Incorporated

SETX Beacon was formally incorporated. Leadership structure was put in place and the organization's core mission, values, and programs were defined.

2026

Growing Steadily

Now focused on steady growth. We host small gatherings, support local efforts, and build relationships across the region.
